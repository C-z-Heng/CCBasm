ABOUT US

Xtree Solutions SDN BHD was established in 2021. Xtree Solutions are an authorized dealer in YGL ERP System, SalesHero & SiteGiant Software and have worked in multiple sectors which include retail, services, restaurants, manufacturers and trading, throughout the years. The business nature of Xtree Solutions is to provide the best care for the customer, and provide information technology consultancy services to customers.
